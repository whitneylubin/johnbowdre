ABOUT

I started my early career experiences in finance. I conducted financial and strategic due diligence on Hedge Funds and Private Equity Firms for internal marketing to ultra high-net worth clients for several leading investment banks in New York. After the financial crisis, I reacted globally and launched my brand strategy group with a partnership in Shanghai, China. Utilizing this platform, I consulted with small to midsized U.S. enterprises on best practices to expand strategic relationships within China. In addition to analysing and synthesising complex market data to advise clients, I created original branding strategies for local and multinational companies.
